# Chennai Attractions and Culture

Chennai, the capital of Tamil Nadu, is known for its rich cultural heritage, beautiful temples, and vibrant arts scene.

## Attractions
- **Marina Beach**: One of the longest beaches in the world, ideal for a morning walk.
- **Kapaleeshwarar Temple**: A famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, showcasing Dravidian architecture.
- **Government Museum**: Houses a large collection of archaeological and numismatic artifacts.
- **Fort St. George**: The first British fortress in India, now a museum.

## Culture
Chennai is not just about its attractions; it is also steeped in a unique culture enriched by history.
- **Cuisine**: Known for its spicy, flavorful vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes. Don't miss trying the local specialties like Filter Coffee and Dosa!
- **Festivals**: Major festivals include Pongal, Tamil New Year, and Navratri, celebrated with much zeal and enthusiasm.
- **Music and Dance**: Home to classical arts; it hosts the famous Chennai Music Season every December.

For more detailed information about Chennai, visit [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chennai) or [Visit Tamil Nadu](https://www.tamilnadutourism.tn.gov.in/).

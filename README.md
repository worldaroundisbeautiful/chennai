## Understanding UDS: Why It Matters

Before we dive into the properties, let's understand **UDS (Undivided Share of Land)**.

### What is UDS?

When you buy an apartment, you're purchasing two things:
1. **The physical flat** (walls, floors, fixtures)
2. **A share of the land** on which the building stands

This land share is called UDS — it's registered in your name and has no defined boundaries. You co-own the entire land parcel with other residents.

### UDS Calculation Formula

```
UDS = (Your Flat's Built-up Area ÷ Total Built-up Area) × Total Land Area
```

### UDS Benchmarks for Chennai

- ✅ **Excellent**: 45-50% of flat area
- ✅ **Good**: 35-45% of flat area
- ⚠️ **Average**: 25-35% of flat area
- ❌ **Below Average**: <25% of flat area

### Why UDS is Critical

1. **Resale Value**: Higher UDS = Better appreciation over time
2. **Redevelopment**: More land share = Better compensation when the building is redeveloped
3. **Bank Loans**: Banks prefer properties with good UDS
4. **Legal Security**: Clear land ownership rights
